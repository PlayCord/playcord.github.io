Poker

Objective

Win chips by making the best hand at showdown or forcing all opponents to fold.

Typical Round Flow (Texas Hold'em style)

  1. Players post blinds/antes as configured.
  2. Each player receives hole cards.
  3. Betting rounds occur with community cards revealed between rounds.
  4. Remaining players reach showdown and best hand wins the pot.

Common Actions

  • check when no bet to call.
  • call to match current bet.
  • raise to increase the bet.
  • fold to leave the current hand.

Hand Ranking (High to Low)

Straight flush, four of a kind, full house, flush, straight, three of a kind, two pair, pair, high card.

Win Condition

Match format may be one-hand winner or elimination by chip count, depending on bot settings.
