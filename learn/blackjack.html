Blackjack

Objective

Finish with a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer without going over 21.

Card Values

  • Number cards count as face value.
  • Face cards count as 10.
  • Aces count as 1 or 11, whichever is better for the hand.

Turn Flow

  1. Players receive initial cards, dealer receives a visible card and a hidden card.
  2. Choose actions such as hit or stand (and optional actions if enabled).
  3. If your total exceeds 21, you bust and lose that hand.
  4. After all players act, dealer draws by house rules (commonly until 17+).

Win Condition

  • Beat the dealer total without busting.
  • Dealer bust means remaining players win.
  • Equal totals are a push unless custom rules override.

Strategy Tips

  • Avoid hitting hard totals 17+ in standard rules.
  • Use dealer up-card strength to guide risk.
  • Treat soft hands (with an Ace) differently from hard hands.
