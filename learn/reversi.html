Reversi

Objective

Finish with more discs of your color on the board than your opponent.

Legal Move Rule

A move is legal only if it brackets one or more opponent discs between the new disc and another of your discs in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

Turn Flow

  1. Place a disc on a legal square.
  2. All bracketed opponent discs are flipped to your color.
  3. If a player has no legal move, they pass automatically.

End and Scoring

  • Game ends when neither player can move or board is full.
  • Count discs. Higher count wins.

Strategy Tips

  • Prioritize corners; they are stable and cannot be flipped.
  • Avoid giving opponent easy corner access.
  • In midgame, mobility is often more important than raw disc count.
