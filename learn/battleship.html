Battleship

Objective

Sink all enemy ships before your opponent sinks yours.

Setup

  • 2 players.
  • Each player has a hidden grid with ships placed on valid coordinates.
  • Ships usually cannot overlap and must stay within board boundaries.

Turn Flow

  1. Choose one target coordinate on the opponent board.
  2. The game reports hit, miss, or sunk.
  3. Play passes to the other player unless your implementation grants bonus shots (check server config).

Win Condition

You win immediately when all opponent ships are sunk.

Strategy Tips

  • Use a checkerboard search pattern to maximize coverage.
  • After a hit, switch to focused targeting around neighboring cells.
  • Track misses to avoid repeating low-value lines.
