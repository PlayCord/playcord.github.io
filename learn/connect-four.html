Connect Four

Objective

Be the first player to connect four of your pieces in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.

Setup

  • 2 players on a vertical grid.
  • Each turn, choose a column to drop one piece.

Turn Flow

  1. Select a non-full column.
  2. Your piece occupies the lowest open cell in that column.
  3. Check for a four-in-a-row, otherwise turn passes.

Win and Draw

  • Win by creating a line of four.
  • If the board fills with no line of four, the game is a draw.

Strategy Tips

  • Center columns are typically stronger.
  • Create double threats that force one-block responses.
  • Avoid giving your opponent an immediate winning move.
