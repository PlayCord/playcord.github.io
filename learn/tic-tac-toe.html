Tic-Tac-Toe

Objective

Be the first player to place three marks in a row.

Rules

  • 2 players alternate turns on a 3x3 grid.
  • You may place a mark only in an empty cell.
  • A line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

End Conditions

  • Player with first completed line wins.
  • If all cells are filled with no completed line, game is a draw.

Strategy Tips

  • Take center when available.
  • Create forks to force multiple threats.
  • Block opponent lines early.
