No Thanks!

Objective

Finish with the lowest score by managing your chips and taking efficient card runs.

Turn Choices

  1. On your turn, either take the face-up card or pay one chip to pass.
  2. If you pass, add your chip to that card and turn passes.
  3. If you take the card, you also collect all chips on it.

Scoring

  • Only the lowest card in each consecutive run counts toward score.
  • Each remaining chip usually reduces your score by 1.
  • Lower total score is better.

End of Game

Game ends when deck is exhausted or round limit is reached. Lowest score wins.

Strategy Tips

  • Pass expensive isolated cards when possible.
  • Take cards that connect into existing runs.
  • Use chips to pressure opponents into bad pickups.
