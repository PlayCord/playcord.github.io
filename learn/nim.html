Nim

Objective

Force your opponent into the losing final move in the configured Nim variant.

Setup

  • 2 players.
  • One or more piles of objects are displayed.

Turn Flow

  1. Choose exactly one pile.
  2. Remove one or more objects from that pile.
  3. Turn passes to the other player.

End Condition

When all piles are empty, winner is decided by the mode your server uses:

  • Normal play: player taking the last object wins.
  • Misere play: player taking the last object loses.

Strategy Tip

In normal play, aim to leave a zero nim-sum after each move.
