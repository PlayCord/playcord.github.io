Liar's Dice

Objective

Outlast all opponents by making accurate bids and successful challenges.

Round Structure

  1. Each player rolls hidden dice.
  2. Starting player makes a bid on total quantity of a face value across all dice.
  3. Next players must raise the bid or call liar (challenge).
  4. On challenge, all dice are revealed and the bid is verified.

Bid Rules

  • A valid raise increases quantity, or value according to your bot ordering rules.
  • Some variants treat ones as wild; check your server settings.

Losing Dice

The loser of a challenge typically loses one die. A player with no dice is eliminated.

Win Condition

The last player with at least one die remaining wins the match.
